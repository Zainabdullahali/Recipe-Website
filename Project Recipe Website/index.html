Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup full-fat milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 5 to 6 tablespoons milk
  • Oil for deep-frying

Directions

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Let the dough rest
  3. Prepare the sugar syrup
  4. Roll the jamuns
  5. Fry the jamuns
  6. Add jamuns to the sugar syrup

Milk Barfi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 300 ml condensed milk
  • 21/2 cup milk powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 handful almonds & pistachios

Directions

  1. Make the milk powder dough
  2. Grate the dough
  3. heat some ghee
  4. Cook the grated dough with cardamom
  5. Garnish with silver varq
  6. Garnish with almonds before serving
